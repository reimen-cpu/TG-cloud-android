Android application to manage files in the cloud using Telegram as backend.

**Main Features:**

*   **No Limits:** Unlimited storage and file size (chunked upload).
*   **File Management:** Native upload, download and organization.
*   **Media Gallery:** Integrated image and video viewer with streaming.
*   **Security:** Encrypted database (SQLCipher) and custom bot support.
*   **Share:** Generate .link files to share content securely.
*   **Privacy:** Your data is stored in your own Telegram chats ("Saved Messages").

**Requirements:**
*   Own Telegram Bot (token from @BotFather).
*   Android 9.0+.

**Note:** This application is not official from Telegram and uses the Telegram Bot API.
